Pomodoro App is based on the famous Pomodoro Technique by Franchesco Cirillo.
It allows to use the brain most efficiently. 

The sense of it is in the splitting of work for a smaller tasks and time pieces:
25 min work
5 min break
25 min work
5 min break
25 min work
5 min break
25 min work
20 min min break

Enjoy and work even more efficiently!
